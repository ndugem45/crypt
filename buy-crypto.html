Buy & Sell Crypto

Users can easily buy Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies using a wide range of payment options, including bank transfer, credit or debit card, and cash. There’s a payment option for everyone on ByteDex.

We work only with verified and trusted partners to give you a secure and seamless crypto-buying experience. ByteDex accepts a wide range of currencies and makes it easy for you to buy crypto using US Dollars, Euros, Pound Sterling, and other fiat currencies. You can also use a wide range of accepted stablecoins such as Binance USD (BUSD), Coinbase USD Coin (USDC), PAXOS (PAX), Tether (USDT), and True USD to buy crypto.

Once you complete the purchase, we will deposit your new crypto directly to your Binance wallet - a safe and simple way to manage your crypto assets. You can immediately trade your purchased crypto on the many products and services on the ByteDex.
